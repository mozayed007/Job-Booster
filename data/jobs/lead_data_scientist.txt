Job Description:
We're looking for a Lead Data Scientist to join our AI/ML Applications team. In this role, you'll spearhead the development of sophisticated machine learning models that power our predictive analytics platform. You'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business problems, design data science solutions, and drive implementation from concept to production.

Responsibilities:
• Lead the development of machine learning models and algorithms to solve complex business problems
• Design and implement end-to-end data science pipelines from data collection to model deployment
• Translate business requirements into technical specifications for data science projects
• Conduct exploratory data analysis to uncover patterns, trends, and insights
• Evaluate and select appropriate modeling techniques, feature engineering approaches, and validation methods
• Collaborate with data engineers to optimize data pipelines and processing workflows
• Work with software engineers to integrate models into production systems
• Mentor junior data scientists and provide technical guidance to the team
• Stay current with cutting-edge research and technologies in AI/ML
• Communicate findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical stakeholders
