# Interview Coach

Generates tailored interview preparation material:
- Behavioral questions covering leadership, conflict, failure, teamwork, ownership, growth
- Technical topics and likely questions based on the role's tech stack
- Role-specific questions tied to the company/product
- STAR stories extracted from the resume with clear metrics
- Actionable preparation tips

All questions and stories are grounded in the candidate's actual experience and the job description — no generic or fabricated content.
